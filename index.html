Review of Unfinished Business from Last meeting

In the last session, the committee addressed several critical issues that remain unresolved and require further discussion or final decision-making. Discussions on potential research collaborations with nearby tech companies were initiated but did not reach a conclusion. More information on partnership terms and intellectual property rights is required. Plans to expand our community outreach programs have stalled due to unforeseen budget constraints. The board needs to revisit funding options to ensure these initiatives can continue.

New Business

In our upcoming Arduino Workshop, participants will have the opportunity to delve into the basics of microcontroller programming and electronic circuit design. The workshop will be led by Dr. Ali Rohan, an expert in robotic systems. Participants will engage in hands-on activities designed to demonstrate how Arduino can be used to create interactive projects and automation solutions.

In conclusion, the board emphasizes the urgency of revising our current curriculum to include more practical, hands-on experiences. These updates are crucial to maintaining our accreditation and attracting prospective students.

Miscellaneous
